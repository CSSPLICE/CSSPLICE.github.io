Collin Lynch
Transaction-Based Analysis of Piazza and other learning platforms

In this talk I will briefly describe our analysis of data from online learning platforms with a special attention to our work with Piazza. I will also describe our shared library for Piazza data analysis, itself the product of a CSSPLICE grant. And I will start the discussion for our initiative to share standards for the analysis of transactional data from educational platforms.
