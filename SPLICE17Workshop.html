SPLICE 2017 Workshop

The first SPLICE workshop has been held in Pittsburgh on June 5-6, 2017.

Workshop Theme: Community-building for data-intensive computer and computing science education infrastructure research

Workshop Hosts:
  • Cliff Shaffer, Virginia Tech
  • Peter Brusilovsky, U Pitt
  • Ken Koedinger, CMU
  • Steve Edwards, Virginia Tech

Abstract:
The workshop attempted to bring together researchers working on different aspects of interoperability, data collection and data analysis in computer science education. Over 25 people attended the workshop. The schedule included presentations and working group discussion. The materials of the workshop are available on this page.
