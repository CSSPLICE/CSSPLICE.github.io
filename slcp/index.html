SPLICE Working Group: Smart Learning Content Protocols

Leaders: Leaders: Cay Horstmann, Steve Edwards.

Rationale Standard learning system APIs such as LTI are complex and often incorporate server-side components. Too often systems that integrate smart learning content use proprietary APIs that restrict interoperability. This proposal aims to enable learning systems to make use of compliant smart content by lowering the cost of entry through simple minimalistic APIs.
