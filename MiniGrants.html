SPLICE Mini-Grants Program

Both the original NSF grant and the current NSF grant include funding for SPLICE to provide mini-grants (typically, $5,000-10,000) to our collaborators to do work that advances SPLICE priorities. Below is information on prior and ongoing mini-grant projects, and also information on how to apply for a mini-grant.

Apply for a SPLICE Mini-Grant

To apply, send a brief proposal (1-2 pages) including title, personnel, and budget to Peter Brusilovsky (peterb@pitt.edu).
