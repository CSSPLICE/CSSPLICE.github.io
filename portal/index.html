SPLICE Working Group: SPLICE Portal

Leaders: (Cliff Shaffer is organizing this for now, need more official leaders!)

Rationale: A group to help set standards for and guide the growth of the SPLICE Portal. The Portal is intended to be a primary deliverable of the SPLICE Project. This should provide access to community-curated content and data. Currently, the primary expression of the Portal is through the SPLICE Catalog.
